Entrypal Admin Dashboard
for eventcreators.

If you are an events creator or planner, and you want to issue tickets for your events digitally, and at the same time see tickets sales real time and withdraw earnings instantly, then the entrypal dashboard for event creators is for you.

You will have access to a Issue tickets page - to issue tickets, set prices and details; View analytics of issued tickets campaign, money realised, number of users, contact details, a Wallet page to instantly withdraw your tickets earnings; and a Analytics/Reports/Statements page - see valuable data and reports from your previous ticketing issue and events campaign.

Entrypal Mobile App
for eventgoers

For every event, whether a party, concert, conference etc, as an eventgoer - all you need is download the app and purchase your ticket in app.

You would have access to a Newfeed to see available/upcoming events;a purchase page to seamlessly pay for tickets through wallet balances or credit cards or other internet payment channels; a Notifications page to keep track of events, past events, upcoming events; a Chat window with fellow eventgoers and share updates; and a Safe Basket page to house all tickets purchased in-app for easy gate pass and event access.
